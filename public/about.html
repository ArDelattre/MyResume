About me

I am a computer science student, passionate about anything IT related. I find it really hard to chose one area to study in more depth, since everything seems so interesting to me. I love the practical aspect of web development, but I always had a soft spot for Cybersecurity and AI. I'd like to think I will get to a point one day where I can start designing my own intelligent agents.

I studied in Australia, but did most of my primary and secondary education around the world. My family gave me the opportunity from a young age to travel the world and familiarise myself with different cultures and individuals from many ethnic backgrounds, which was truly a blessing. I have a tendency to find an interest in what I do not fully comprehend, which keeps me curious and humble. Consequently I do not specialise in anything yet, but I am a quick and passionate learner with an optimistic attitude.
